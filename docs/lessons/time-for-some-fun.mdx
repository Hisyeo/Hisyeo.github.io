# Time For Some Fun

In Hîsyêô culture, leisure is not merely the absence of work; it is the active
celebration of life, guided by the god [Kulîôbî](../religion/gods/kulîôbî).
Whether dancing, drinking, or watching a story, "fun" is a serious business of
joy.

## Music & Dancing

Music (kontol) and dance (cifulo) are communal activities.

| English      | Hîsyêô                                   | Literal Meaning     |
|--------------|------------------------------------------|---------------------|
| Music        | <HisyeoWord is='kontol'/>                | to play/sing        |
| Song         | <HisyeoWord is='kontol'/>                | *Noun Form*         |
| To Sing      | <HisyeoText>kontol bi ôîtun</HisyeoText> | Make music by voice |
| Dancing      | <HisyeoWord is='cifulo'/>                |                     |
| Instrument   | <HisyeoText>yôn în kontol</HisyeoText>      | Music tool          |
| Band / Group | <HisyeoText>zomo ônî kontol</HisyeoText>     | Player/Singer group        |

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>nûs kûu li cifulo mût kûon kut yê lono ze!&emsp;<em>Let's dance until dawn!</em></p>
    <p>û kontol onô li cot êto.&emsp;<em>The song is very long (thick).</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>

## Movies & Stories

Visual storytelling is a popular pastime.

| English          | Hîsyêô                                  | Literal Meaning |
|------------------|-----------------------------------------|-----------------|
| Movie / Film     | <HisyeoText>non</HisyeoText>       |     |
| Story / Plot     | <HisyeoWord is='kîso'/>                 | Narrative       |
| Theater / Cinema | <HisyeoText>îlê non</HisyeoText>        | Show building   |
| Actor            | <HisyeoText>ônî wêîlun non</HisyeoText> | Show behaver    |

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>nîmû fôlun xe dêko non ko?&emsp;<em>Do you want to watch a movie?</em></p>
    <p>û kîso onô li foko sunyotnî lôîcôk.&emsp;<em>The story was so interesting.</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>

## Drinking & Bars

Alcohol is often enjoyed in a gubuyo îskô (Drink Store / Pub).

| English      | Hîsyêô                                    | Literal Meaning       |
|--------------|-------------------------------------------|-----------------------|
| Hard Alcohol | <HisyeoText>îskô în cunlîû</HisyeoText>   | Distilled drink       |
| Beer         | <HisyeoText>îskô cûbû kûkmut</HisyeoText> | Grain fermented drink |
| Wine         | <HisyeoText>îskô cûbû kûôxi</HisyeoText>  | Fruit fermented drink |
| Bar / Pub    | <HisyeoText>gubuyo kon nexê</HisyeoText>  | Intoxication store    |
| Cheers!      | <HisyeoText>sohoû nûs!</HisyeoText>       | Our health!           |
| Drunk        | <HisyeoText>nexê</HisyeoText>             |                       |

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>nûs kûu xe dôstoc îskô cûbû hoî gubuyo.&emsp;<em>Let's get a drink at the pub.</em></p>
    <p>noî sûtûô nêôs mût cîgîlsu nîmû ze.&emsp;<em>Our new friend, to your health!</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>

## Smoking

Smoking is a social habit for some, often involving communal sharing.

| English          | Hîsyêô                              | Literal Meaning |
|------------------|-------------------------------------|-----------------|
| Smoke            | <HisyeoWord is='wôlik'/>            | Cloud / Smoke   |
| To Smoke         | <HisyeoText>wedo wôlik (til nexê)</HisyeoText> | To exchange smoke for intoxicated  |
| Cigarette / Pipe | <HisyeoText>nûyô kon nexê</HisyeoText>   | Intoxication stick       |
| Fire / Light     | <HisyeoWord is='môtô'/>             | Fire            |

## Parties

Parties (kôndodû) are the ultimate expression of Tîengûun's community.

| English | Hîsyêô                                | Literal Meaning       |
|---------|---------------------------------------|-----------------------|
| Party   | <HisyeoWord is='kôndodû'/>            | to celebrate          |
| Feast   | <HisyeoText>côûfon cênbô</HisyeoText> | Big meal              |
| Guest   | <HisyeoWord is='monodo'/>             | Visitor / Meeting-one |
| Host    | <HisyeoText>ônî niwos</HisyeoText>    | Residing one          |

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>û kôndodû ke li gô hoî niwos noyo.&emsp;<em>The party will be at my house.</em></p>
    <p>kûu li gîk ofek îskô cizî.&emsp;<em>Bring your own booze.</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>
