# Small Talk

Like all humans, Hîsyêô conversations can sometimes seem to just involve these
details about our lives, our families and the weather.

## How Are You?

The most basic form of small talk is asking how someone is feeling. This shows
the receiver of the question that you are interested in them and their
wellbeing.

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>bi zik û nîmû li gô hoî lono ôfo?&emsp;<em>How are you doing today?</em></p>
    <p>oncêmon zik û nîmû hoî îbûdû?&emsp;<em>How are you feeling now?</em></p>
    <p>bi zik û nîmû noî umo Xoônu?&emsp;<em>How are you, Shawna?</em></p>
    <p>nîmû li xôn noî umo Lendun ko.&emsp;<em>Are you okay, Landon?</em></p>
    <p>ujo (li gô) bi zik noî umo Sên?.&emsp;<em>How are things, Zane?</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>

## What Are You Doing?

Another common topic of conversation is what someone is doing, idiomatically
what they are "up to". There are three forms that are a good fit for this question.

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>(nîmû) cînlî zik noî umo Donî?&emsp;<em>What are you up to, Donny?</em></p>
    <p>noî umo Lôdolfô (û nîmû) tûu zik?&emsp;<em>Rodolfo, what are you doing?</em></p>
    <p>zik li gô noî umo Kefulin?&emsp;<em>What's happening, Katheryn?</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>

## Health & Wellness

Often times we also ask if someone is feeling good or tell others about a recent
sickness we experienced.

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>nîmû oncêmon zôtêstô (sohoû) hoî lono ôfo noî umo Sokuloyo ko.&emsp;<em>Are you feeling okay today, Zaccaria?</em></p>
    <p>noî umo Wûon û nîmû dôstoc sobûnî ko.&emsp;<em>Juan, did you get sick?</em></p>
    <p>noyo oncêmon yôk xôn noî Nôê.&emsp;<em>I am not feeling well, Noe.</em></p>
    <p>umo Yûlî li sobûnî lôîcôk bînê nîo buswen xe nocîdo wêloi hoî lono ôfo.&emsp;<em>Yury is so sick that he had to skip work today.</em></p>
    <p>umo Ulsenî oncêmon êto xôn eswen bînê nûs cînlî côûfon mutyu ofek nîo hoî lono xobêk.&emsp;<em>Arseny is feeling much better that we had lunch with her yesterday.</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>

## Loss & Condolences

Unfortunately, we all lose loved ones at some point. Here are some different
expressions and responses you can use. 

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>nîmû hûsku kukwon zomo kon bêûdo nûs Bulônîswofo ko. nîo bi ubênus dî si tîkû.&emsp;<em>Do you remember our classmate Bronisława? She just passed away. </em></p>
    <p>motûo wohî noyo dî sî tîkû hoî wokût ôhêl mônbili.&emsp;<em>My mom died a while ago.</em></p>
    <p>motûo moskûl nîmû bi kônyêc dî sî tîkû ko.&emsp;<em>Did your father finally pass?</em></p>
    <p>dî.&emsp;<em>Yes, he did.</em></p>
    <p>kut umo fos noyo monodo, bi boyok dî sî tîkû ko.&emsp;<em>Did someone you know recently die?</em></p>
    <p>mênyûu noyo (bi boyok dî si tîkû).&emsp;<em>My daughter recently died.</em></p>
    <p>fos mênyûu dî tîkû, kôi li êo kobût mûlû ôlê motûo.&emsp;<em>When a child passes, it can destroy the parents.</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>

Here are some things you can say to show your condolences.

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>ôlê noyo bi gîôn li êo kûyô û xe tenkîn kon nocîdo nîmû ze.&emsp;<em>I am extremely saddened from hearing about your loss!</em></p>
    <p>(noyo) (mût nîmû) (cûdo) kon zûtô yê zîlyoîs môî et noyo til kon nocîdo êto nîmû.&emsp;<em>My deepest sympathy at your great loss.</em></p>
    <p>kûu fisun fos noyo hofo til nîmû lôtfen.&emsp;<em>Please know that I am praying for you.</em></p>
    <p>(noyo) kôî wêko yôk cizî hoî êtsô nîmû.&emsp;<em>I cannot put myself in your situation.</em></p>
    <p>(noyo) fisun fos li xoînbo û fos uyo li odigon kutin hoî îbûdû cônkôk.&emsp;<em>I know things seem too difficult right now.</em></p>
    <p>kûu dênêmêk xe ogolol xîwon.&emsp;<em>Try to hang on to hope.</em></p>
    <p>(mût noyo) kûu hobîyô hôn fos nîmû bît buswen xe kôto yodo kut umo fos li êo nonto ofek.&emsp;<em>Let me know if you need to talk or just someone to cry with.</em></p>
    <p>(noyo) fisun fos li xûnyu fos noyo kôî kôto til fos ôlê tônkô nîmû li êo fonmot.&emsp;<em>I know there is nothing I can say to make your pain go away.</em></p>
    <p>kûu fisun fos noyo hoî môî wokût ke li gô hoî ulyô til nîmû.&emsp;<em>Please know that I will always be there for you.</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>

## Weather

It's also common to talk about the weather since meteorologists don't seem to
know what they're doing anymore.

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>bi zik û kêlôs li sî gô hoî boxô nîmû?&emsp;<em>How has the weather been in your neck of the woods?</em></p>
    <p>nîmû fuî sî kônên til xe li gô hoî wokto ko.&emsp;<em>Have you had a chance to get outside today?</em></p>
    <p>kûu wuîtsî lîmîen hôn fos nîmû fôlun xe uskîfo hûzon onô.&emsp;<em>Stay indoors if you want to avoid the rain.</em></p>
    <p>li hûzon bûgot ûn tenkîn bôkbûn lêkin û noyo dêko yôk ozûul kûon bôkbûn.&emsp;<em>There is heavy rain and thunder but I didn't see any lightning.</em></p>
    <p>li sî fûongok yê êto longi cihôû bôkbûn onô xe hoî lono unbol.&emsp;<em>There was a beautiful rainbow after the storm on Saturday.</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>
