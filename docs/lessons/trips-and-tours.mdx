# Trips & Tours

Traveling is more than just movement; it is an act of curiosity ([Suluswutî](../religion/gods/suluswutî)) and
connection ([Tîengûun](../religion/gods/tîêngûun)). When we visit new places, we are guests in someone else's Weave.

## Flying & Air Travel

While we honor the earth, sometimes we must fly like a bird (tîmîok) to cross great distances.

## Cultural Respect

In Hîsyêô culture, visiting a foreign land is an exercise in Empathy ([Bostet](../religion/gods/bostet)'s domain). You are entering a different part of the Great Weave, with its own patterns and logic.

**Listen First:** Use <HisyeoWord is='fôgîcîn'/> (curiosity). Ask questions rather than making judgments.

**Adapt:** Try to follow local customs. <HisyeoText>noyo fôlun xe tûu misli nîmû</HisyeoText> (I want to do as you do).

**Gratitude:** Always show appreciation for hospitality.

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>noyo li monodo ilik kûôcyo nîmû.&emsp;<em>I am a guest (visitor) in your country.</em></p>
    <p>ôlê noyo kûu bêûdo êo kon dondun nîmû.&emsp;<em>Teach me your customs/traditions.</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>
