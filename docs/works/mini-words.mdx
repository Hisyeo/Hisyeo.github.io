# Translations of the Mini Dictionary

Another language that is bigger than [toki pona](https://tokipona.org) and
[Kokanu](https://en.kokanu.com) but smaller than natural languages is called
[Mini](https://minilanguage.com). It is similar to Hîsyêô in its origin as a
[tokiponido](https://sona.pona.la/wiki/Tokiponidos) but with a completely
different direction from Hîsyêô in terms of grammar and word sourcing (keep in
mind that Hîsyêô's journey has taken it significantly far from [toki
ma](https://sites.google.com/view/toki-ma/toki-maq), the original tokiponido
that Kokanu was born from). Here are all of the words of the language translated
into Hîsyêô.

:::info Lexicalized Compounds

Hîsyêô compounds are not set in stone or lexicalized at the current time. This
is partly due to the fact that the Hîsyêô community has not yet fully formed but
also due to a desire from the creators to keep compounds lexically loose. Small
languages are more powerful when lexicalization is prevented so that the total
lexicon size doesn't increase over time.

That means that the compounds offered below are only just *possible* options,
you can come up with something more specific, more minimal or a completely
different approach. The limit is only what will be understandable to those you
are speaking with. Hopefully someday, this list will be updated with the more
common compounds that are used in day-to-day speech.

:::
