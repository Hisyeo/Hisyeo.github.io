# Sociology

Sociology is the study of zomî (society) and zomo (groups). In Hîsyêô, sociology
is not just an academic observation but a tool for constructing the Great
Weave—a society based on voluntary association rather than coercion.

## Social Dynamics

How do groups interact and change?

**Solidarity:** <HisyeoText>kotilmok yoldûn yê umo dîzel</HisyeoText> (Aid contributing of other people)

**Conflict:** <HisyeoWord is='gûexo'/>

**Cooperation:** <HisyeoText>kotilmok xe til xokôn dîzel</HisyeoText> (Working each other/together)

**Assimilation:** <HisyeoText>kon gomî yê dondun</HisyeoText> (Taking of culture) - Often viewed negatively as erasing multiplicity.

**Integration:** <HisyeoText>kon ûnîdû yê cizî losol</HisyeoText> (Joining of original self) - Viewed positively as adding threads to the Weave.
