# Physics

Physics (<HisyeoWord is='bêûdo'/> <HisyeoWord is='ûlzo'/>) is the study of <HisyeoWord is='ûlzo'/> (force) and the fundamental rules of the [Material Dream](../religion/metaphysics#the-material). In Hîsyêô, physics is the study of the **Benevolent Remnants**—the stable laws left behind by the First Law that allow the universe to exist.

| Physics     | <HisyeoWord is='bêûdo'/> <HisyeoWord is='ûlzo'/> | Study of force           |
| Force       | <HisyeoWord is='ûlzo'/>                          | Energy / Power           |

## Classical Mechanics

The study of motion and forces.

<HisyeoText><strong>Motion:</strong>nô&emsp;<small>(Movement)</small></HisyeoText>

<HisyeoText><strong>Velocity:</strong>nô xokôn&emsp;<small>(Unit of movement)</small></HisyeoText>

<HisyeoText><strong>Mass:</strong>kûsowî xokôn&emsp;<small>(Unit of matter)</small></HisyeoText>

<HisyeoText><strong>Gravity:</strong>ûlzo zîgîl&emsp;<small>(Falling force)</small></HisyeoText>

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>Gûdolô zîgîl li ostok yê fûtsûn kut.&emsp;<em>Gravity is a sign of the First Law (The Benevolent Remnant).</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>

## Thermodynamics

The study of heat (<HisyeoWord is='tût'/>) and energy transfer (<HisyeoWord is='kon'/> <HisyeoWord is='wedo'/> <HisyeoWord is='ûlzo'/>), heavily linked to [Hûlicîn](../religion/gods/hûlicîn) (Entropy).

**Heat:** <HisyeoWord is='tût'/>&emsp;<small>(Heat/Hot)</small>

**Cold:** <HisyeoWord is='cunyû'/>&emsp;<small>(Cold)</small>

**Temperature:** <HisyeoWord is='tût'/> <HisyeoWord is='xokôn'/>&emsp;<small>(Unit of heat)</small>

**Entropy:** <HisyeoWord is='hoôs'/>&emsp;<small>(Chaos/Entropy)</small>

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>ûlzo li nô sûn tût mût cunyû.&emsp;<em>Energy moves from hot to cold.</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>

## Electromagnetism

The study of light and charge.

**Electricity:** <HisyeoWord is='cingî'/>

**Light:** <HisyeoWord is='kûon'/>

<HisyeoText><strong>Current:</strong> xûto cingî&emsp;<small>(Electrical flow)</small></HisyeoText>

<HisyeoText><strong>Circuit:</strong> tîton în ûnhilun yê xûto cingî&emsp;<small>(Enclosed path of electrical flow)</small></HisyeoText>

<HisyeoText><strong>Charge:</strong> mîkdol cingî&emsp;<small>(Electrical amount)</small></HisyeoText>

<HisyeoText><strong>Magnet:</strong>dîncî foko nô&emsp;<small>(Stone agent of causing moving)</small></HisyeoText>

## Quantum Mechanics

The study of the very small, where the [Divine Mind](../religion/metaphysics#the-divine-mind) is perhaps visible.

<HisyeoText><strong>Atom/Particle:</strong>folo</HisyeoText>

**Wave:** <HisyeoWord is='noô'/>&emsp;<small>(Wave/Undulation)</small>

<HisyeoText><strong>Quantum particles:</strong> folo yê cûtî môî&emsp;<small>(Smallest particles)</small></HisyeoText>

<HisyeoText><strong>Entanglement:</strong> kon môtsilê hoôs yê folo yê cûtî môî sûn mônbili êto&emsp;<small>(Chaotic acts of tying of the smallest particles from great distances)</small></HisyeoText>

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>li hoôs ilik folo.&emsp;<em>There is chaos in the atom.</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>
