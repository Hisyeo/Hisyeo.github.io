# Introduction

In Hîsyêô, the pursuit of knowledge is known as <HisyeoWord is='bêûdo'/>. While
the [First Law](../religion/first-law.mdx) shattered the universe into chaos,
science is the method by which we observe the
[Shards](../religion/shattering.mdx#the-shards) and the [Cosmic
Soul](../religion/metaphysics#the-cosmic-soul) to understand the rules of the
[Material Dream](../religion/metaphysics#the-material).

## The Disciplines

Scientific fields are generally constructed as <HisyeoWord is='bêûdo'/> + <HisyeoWord is='yê'/> + [Topic].

| Discipline  | Hîsyêô              | Literal Meaning         |
|-------------|---------------------|-------------------------|
| [Mathematics](mathematics) | <HisyeoText>sôhoûk             </HisyeoText> |                         |
| [Biology](biology)     | <HisyeoText>bêûdo yê zîwit     </HisyeoText> | Study of Life           |
| [Physics](physics)     | <HisyeoText>bêûdo yê ûlzo      </HisyeoText> | Study of Power/Forces   |
| [Technology](technology)  | <HisyeoText>bêûdo yê culono    </HisyeoText> | Study of Machines       |
| [Sociology](sociology)   | <HisyeoText>bêûdo yê zomo/zomî </HisyeoText> | Study of Groups/Society |
| [Politics](politics)    | <HisyeoText>bêûdo yê kon bôklôn</HisyeoText> | Study of Governance     |
| [History](history)     | <HisyeoText>itihos             </HisyeoText> |                         |
| [Psychology](psychology)  | <HisyeoText>bêûdo yê dûuncît   </HisyeoText> | Study of Mind           |
| [Philosophy](philosophy)  | <HisyeoText>bêûdo yê colot     </HisyeoText> | Study of Knowledge      |

## The Scientific Method

The process of science involves a cycle of observation and testing.

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>dêko&emsp;<small>(To see/observe)</small></p>
    <p>onkonu&emsp;<small>(To estimate/hypothesize)</small></p>
    <p>dênêmêk&emsp;<small>(To test/experiment)</small></p>
    <p>sobis&emsp;<small>(To record)</small></p>
    <p>fohoso&emsp;<small>(To examine/analyze)</small></p>
    <p>fisun&emsp;<small>(To know/conclude)</small></p>
    <p>nûs dênêmêk til xe fisun hokîkî.&emsp;<em>We experiment to know the truth.</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>
