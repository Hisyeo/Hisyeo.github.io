# Medicine

Medicine (<HisyeoWord is='bêûdo'/> <HisyeoWord is='cîgîlsu'/>) is the study of cîgîlsu (<HisyeoWord is='sohoû'/>) and the care of the <HisyeoWord is='solîlo'/> (<HisyeoWord is='body'/>). In Hîsyêô, medicine is a practical discipline of maintenance and repair, guided by [Bostet](../religion/gods/bostet) (Care) and [Unonsî](../religion/gods/unonsî) (Technology).

## Core Concepts

Health is viewed as a state of balance and function within the physical system.

| English            | Hîsyêô                                              | Literal Meaning       |
|--------------------|-----------------------------------------------------|-----------------------|
| Medicine (Field)   | <HisyeoWord is='bêûdo'/> <HisyeoWord is='yê'/> <HisyeoWord is='cîgîlsu'/> | Study of treatments   |
| Health             | <HisyeoWord is='sohoû'/>                            | Healthiness           |
| Body               | <HisyeoWord is='solîlo'/>                           | Body / Corporeal form |
| Disease / Sickness | <HisyeoWord is='sobûnî'/>                           | Sickness / Illness    |
| Pain               | <HisyeoWord is='tônkô'/>                            | Pain / Suffering      |
| Treatment          | <HisyeoWord is='cîgîlsu'/>                          |                       |
| Recovery           | <HisyeoWord is='xîûfû'/>                            | Repair / Restoration  |

## Philosophy of Care

Hîsyêô rejects the concept of "magical healing." The body is a complex biological machine that requires active maintenance.

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>nûs kôs yôk mozîkô; nûs tûu kon xîûfû hokîkî.&emsp;<em>We do not make magic; we do real repair.</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>

## Medical Practice

The medical profession is dedicated to the diagnosis and repair of biological systems.

**Doctor:** <HisyeoText>ônî cûdo cîgîlsu&emsp;<small>(The treatment giving one)</small></HisyeoText>

**Nurse:** <HisyeoText>ônî yoldûn cîgîlsu&emsp;<small>(The treatment helping one)</small></HisyeoText>

**Patient:** <HisyeoText>ônî sobûnî&emsp;<small>(The sick one)</small></HisyeoText>

**Condition:** <HisyeoText>êtsô sobûnî&emsp;<small>(Sick state)</small></HisyeoText>

**Diagnosis:** <HisyeoText>xe yosten sobûnî&emsp;<small>(To discover sickness)</small></HisyeoText>

**Surgeon:** <HisyeoText>ônî xîûfû xofô&emsp;<small>(Guts repairer)</small></HisyeoText>

## Pharmacology

Medications are viewed as tools (<HisyeoWord is='yôn'/>) for the body.

**Medicine (Drug):** <HisyeoText>kûsowî cîgîlsu&emsp;<small>(Treatment material)</small></HisyeoText>

**Pill:** <HisyeoText>gundolê cîgîlsu cûtî&emsp;<small>(Small treatment ball)</small></HisyeoText>

**Vaccine:** <HisyeoText>cîgîlsu ônî cûfo yê yoûtî sobûnî&emsp;<small>(Guardian treatment of some illnesses)</small></HisyeoText>

**Poison:** <HisyeoWord is='nozî'/> (Toxin)

<HisyeoTabs>
    <p>kûsowî cîgîlsu cûdo yoldûn mût solîlo til xe xîûfû cizî.&emsp;<em>Medicine helps the body repair itself.</em></p>
</HisyeoTabs>
